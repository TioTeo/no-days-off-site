Fill out the application below with your gym's details. We partner with independent gyms, boutique studios, and multi-location facilities.

Revenue Model

What a Real Gym Could Earn

The math is straightforward: more trainers, more clients, more revenue. A gym with 5 trainers averaging 10 clients each — all on 1:1 — generates over $985/month in passive gym revenue.

That's not counting Concierge clients, which pay out significantly more per subscription.

There's no cap on trainers, no cap on clients, and no monthly fees to be a gym partner.

Gym Partner Benefits

More Than a Revenue Share

The Gym Partner program comes with tools built for managing multi-trainer operations — not just a check at the end of the month.

🏢

Gym Dashboard

See every trainer in your facility, their active client count, subscription tiers, and revenue contribution — all in one view.

💰

Automated Payouts

Your gym's revenue share is calculated and disbursed automatically every billing cycle. No manual reconciliation. No invoices to chase.

📈

Revenue Analytics

Track monthly revenue trends, trainer performance, and client growth over time. Data that helps you understand what's working.

🤝

Trainer Onboarding

Streamlined onboarding flow gets your trainers set up quickly. New hires can be added to your gym's NDO account from day one.
