Coaching

A Coach, Not an Algorithm

Two types of fitness products exist: ones that give you tools, and ones that give you a coach. Most apps give you tools. NDO gives you both.

Our 1:1 and Concierge members get a dedicated coach who adjusts their programming week over week based on what their body is actually doing — not what a formula predicted at signup. Every week is a new data point. Every adjustment is deliberate.

What We Stand For

  • Daily discipline over motivation. Motivation fluctuates. Systems don't. We build habits that show up whether you feel like it or not.
  • Data over guesswork. Adjustments are made on real numbers — weekly weigh-ins, logged food, training performance. Not assumptions.
  • Coaches, not chatbots. Every coached member works with a real human who knows their history, their goals, and their constraints.
  • Nutrition is training. You can't out-train a bad diet, and you can't sustain results without understanding food. Our Concierge tier treats nutrition as a first-class coaching discipline — not an afterthought.

Nutrition

Nutrition Is Half the Work.

Most coaching programs treat nutrition as an afterthought — a set of macros you get once and never revisit. We don't.

Concierge members get a custom macro prescription that's adjusted every week based on what their body is actually doing — scale trends, energy levels, training output. Their coach reviews all of it and makes the call. That ongoing responsiveness is what separates a prescription from a target.
