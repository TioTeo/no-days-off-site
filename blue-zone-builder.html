Common Questions

What you need to know.

What exactly is a fitness and nutrition audit? +
The NDO audit is a structured diagnostic of your current physical performance and nutritional habits. For fitness, we assess your movement quality, training history, recovery patterns, and output metrics. For nutrition, we analyze your current intake, macro distribution, meal timing, and any gaps relative to your goals. You receive a written NDO Blueprint — a personalized document mapping exactly what to adjust, optimize, and build on.
How is NDO different from hiring a personal trainer? +
A personal trainer typically programs workouts. NDO audits your entire health architecture — fitness, nutrition, recovery, and lifestyle design — and connects them all within the Blue Zone longevity framework. You also get a community of peers operating at the same level, and the NDO app as your daily implementation tool. It's the difference between a single service and a complete operating system.
Do I need to be already fit to join? +
No. The audit meets you exactly where you are. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or someone rebuilding from scratch, the Blueprint is built around your current state. Many of our most motivated members are high performers who are successful in every area of life but have neglected their health systematically — and are ready to change that.
What is the NDO app, and is it included? +
The NDO app is a fitness and nutrition tracking platform built specifically to support the NDO methodology. It's your daily home base between coaching calls and audits. All membership tiers include app access — full unlock is available on Build and Architect tiers.
Can I pay with Bitcoin or crypto? +
Yes. NDO accepts Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDC, USDT, and most major cryptocurrencies in addition to all major credit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ACH bank transfers. Select your preferred method at checkout.
Is there a Spanish-language version? +
Not yet — but it's coming. NDO V2 will launch a fully Spanish-language membership community, with the Nicoya Peninsula Blue Zone as a central pillar of the program. If you're interested in early access, join the waitlist.
